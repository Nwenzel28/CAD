Our Mission

Built by engineers, for engineers who want to go further.

There's a frustrating gap in how CAD is taught. Tutorials teach you the software. Classes teach you theory. But nobody shows you what happens in between — the decisions, the failures, the constraints that define real engineering work.

FormFactor exists to fill that gap. It's a structured learning platform built around the premise that intermediate engineers learn best from real-world examples, documented honestly — including the parts that didn't work.

Every module connects to a case study. Every case study references the resources that powered it. The platform is built to work as a connected learning journey, not a pile of isolated lessons.

Engineering isn't just knowing the tools. It's knowing which question to ask when something doesn't work — and having enough context to answer it.
